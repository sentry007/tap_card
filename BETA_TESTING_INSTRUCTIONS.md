### Step 1: Access the Beta
1. Click the invite link you received (sent separately for security)
2. Sign in with your Google account
3. Tap **"Accept Invitation"**
4. Follow the on-screen instructions to download and install the app
5. If prompted, enable **"Install from unknown sources"** for Firebase App Distribution

1. Check your email for the Firebase App Distribution invitation
2. Click **"Get started"** in the email
3. Sign in with the same email address you were invited with
4. Install the **Firebase App Distribution** app if prompted
5. Download and install **Atlas Linq**

### Step 2: Enable NFC (Required)
1. Go to your phone's **Settings**
2. Search for **"NFC"**
3. Toggle it **ON**
4. Ensure **"Android Beam"** or **"NFC sharing"** is also enabled

## ‚ö†Ô∏è Known Issues & Limitations

### Device Compatibility
- **Minimum Android version**: Android 8.0 (Oreo) or higher
- **NFC required**: Your device must have NFC hardware
- **P2P mode**: Works between Android devices with NFC, and can share web cards with iOS users

### Current Limitations
- **iOS not supported**: Currently Android-only (iOS coming later)
- **Background images**: Large images may take time to load
- **Offline mode**: Some features require internet connection

### Troubleshooting

**NFC not working?**
1. Ensure NFC is enabled in settings
2. Remove phone case (metal cases block NFC)
3. Hold phones back-to-back for 2-3 seconds
4. Try the "More sharing options" ‚Üí QR code method instead

**App crashed?**
- Restart the app - crash report sent automatically
- Check for app updates in Firebase App Distribution

**Profile not saving?**
- Ensure you tapped the "Save Profile" button
- Check that you filled in at least the Name field
